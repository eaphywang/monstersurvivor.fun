About Monster Survivor

Monster Survivor is an action-packed survival game that tests your combat skills and strategic thinking. Face waves of increasingly difficult monsters as you fight to survive in a hostile world. How long can you last against the monster onslaught?

Collect powerful weapons, upgrade your abilities, and master different characters to achieve the highest scores. With its addictive gameplay and endless challenges, Monster Survivor will keep you coming back for more!

Power-ups & Weapons

Challenging Bosses

Global Leaderboards

How to Play

  • 1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map.
  • 2. Your character attacks automatically when monsters are within range.
  • 3. Collect experience gems from defeated monsters to level up.
  • 4. When you level up, choose from various upgrades to enhance your abilities.
  • 5. Avoid getting hit by monsters to prevent losing health.
  • 6. Survive as long as possible and defeat as many monsters as you can!

Tips for Success

Beginner Tips

  • Focus on movement speed upgrades early on
  • Don't stop moving to avoid getting surrounded
  • Prioritize health regeneration when available
  • Learn each monster's attack pattern

Advanced Strategies

  • Balance offensive and defensive upgrades
  • Use environmental obstacles to your advantage
  • Save power-ups for boss encounters
  • Experiment with different character builds
